Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our policies

Shipping Information

We offer worldwide shipping on all vehicles. The cost of shipping will be calculated at checkout based on your location and the size of the vehicle. Our standard shipping times are:

  • Domestic Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • International Shipping: 10-15 business days

All vehicles are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. We provide tracking information so you can monitor your shipment.

Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the vehicle within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. To be eligible for a return, the vehicle must be in the same condition that you received it, with all original packaging and documentation.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions on how to send your vehicle back to us.

Once we receive the returned vehicle and confirm it is in its original condition, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days. Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.
